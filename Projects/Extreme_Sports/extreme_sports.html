What is Extreme Sports?

Extreme sports are activities that involve high risks and adrenaline. From skydiving and rock climbing to snowboarding and bungee jumping, these sports push your limits and give an unforgettable thrill.

Fun Facts

  • Skydiving reaches speeds over 120 mph during free fall.
  • Bungee jumping started as a ritual in Vanuatu called "land diving."
  • Rock climbing requires both strength and strategy.
  • Snowboarding evolved from surfing and skateboarding.
